Cyber Bullying Detection System

As part of my capstone project, I developed a Python-based system aimed at detecting and preventing harmful online interactions, promoting safer social media spaces.

  • Technology: Python, NLP, LSTM Models
  • Focus: Detect and block messages containing hate speech or threats.
  • Interactivity: Provides real-time feedback to users.

The system's workflow involves six phases: data collection, preprocessing, classification, testing, evaluation, and comprehensive analysis.

The script efficiently checks inputs against a word bank, providing instant feedback to promote respectful communication.

Data visualizations highlight the prevalence and impact of cyberbullying, illustrating user exposure, incident frequency, and mental health impacts.
